Step 1

Gather Parts and Assemble TERRA


Follow our step-by-step instruction manual to setup TERRA. All relevant CAD files, software dependencies, and setup instructions can be found in our documentation here.




Step 2

Design your Microfluidic Chip Experiment


Once TERRA is assembled, identify the chip you would like to use based on your experiment. Either check out a repository of pre-designed chips here or you can also design your own here using 3DuF. Decide on your final vessel for your microfluidic chip outputs




Step 3

Input Protocol and Run


Head over to Experiment, an easy to use interface that enables you to use TERRA and configure your experiment. Enter the necessary information and select where to dispense. A detailed guide on how to input necessary information can be found here.
